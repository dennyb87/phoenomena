## Problema 9  

$v = 22\pm 0.1\ \small\frac{cm}{s}$  
$s = 45\pm 0.1\ \small\frac{cm}{s}$  

$t_m = \dfrac{s}{v} = \dfrac{45}{22} \simeq 2.045$  

$\varepsilon_r (t) = \varepsilon_r (s) + \varepsilon_r (v) = \dfrac{0.1}{22} + \dfrac{0.1}{45} \simeq 0.0068$  

$\Delta x (t) = \varepsilon_r (t) \cdot t_m = 0.0068 \cdot 2.045 \simeq 0.014 \simeq 0.02 s$  

$t = 2.05\pm 0.02$  

## Problema 10  

$t = 0.91\pm 0.01\ s$  
$s_i = 12.5\pm 0.1\ cm$  
$s_f = 81.4\pm 0.1\ cm$  

$\Delta s_m = s_f - s_i = 68.9\ cm$  
$\Delta x(\Delta s_m) = \Delta x (s_f) + \Delta x (s_i) = 0.1 + 0.1 = 0.2\ cm$  

$\Delta s = 68.9\pm 0.2\ cm$  

$v_m = \dfrac{\Delta s}{t} = \dfrac{68.9}{0.91} = 75.7143\ \small\frac{cm}{s}$  

$\varepsilon_r (v_m) = \varepsilon_r(\Delta s) + \varepsilon_r (t) = \dfrac{0.2}{68.9} + \dfrac{0.01}{0.91} \simeq 0.0139$  

$\Delta x(v) = \varepsilon_r(v_m) \cdot v_m = 0.0139 \cdot 75.7143 = 1.05242877 \simeq 1\ \small\frac{cm}{s}$  

$v = 76\pm 1\ \small\frac{cm}{s}$
